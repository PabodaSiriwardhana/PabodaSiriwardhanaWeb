About Me

I am an ambitious and highly motivated undergraduate student at the University of Colombo School of Computing (UCSC), currently pursuing a Bachelor of Information Technology. My academic journey is fueled by a deep passion for Full-Stack Development, Software Development, Wordpress Development DevOps, and Mobile Application Development, where I continuously strive to expand my expertise and stay ahead of emerging technologies.

With a strong foundation in Full-Stack Development, I have honed my skills in both Front-End and Back-End technologies, working with Frameworks and Tools such as React, Angular, ASP.NET, Spring Boot, Laravel, and Node.js. My programming proficiency spans multiple languages, including Java, JavaScript, TypeScript, C#, PHP, Python, and C, allowing me to build scalable, efficient, and high-performing applications.

Beyond traditional web development, I have a growing interest in mobile app development, particularly in building cross-platform and native applications. I am actively learning frameworks like Flutter and React Native, along with modern mobile UI/UX design principles using Figma to create user-friendly and visually appealing applications.

In addition to software development, I am expanding my knowledge in DevOps, where I am learning about CI/CD pipelines, Containerization with Docker, Kubernetes Orchestration, and Cloud Computing solutions. I recognize the importance of automation, scalability, and reliability in modern software development and am eager to integrate these practices into real-world projects.
